Glass of Water

Recipe source(s): Cookooree, Absolut Drinks

Description

How to make a glass of water.

Requirements

Ingredients
  • Water.
  • Ice (optional).
  • Orange (optional).
Equipment
  • A glass.

Steps

  1. Take a glass.
    • Don't drop it, as it will break and everything you tried for will be ruined!
  2. Fill it with ice cubes, if you wish.
  3. Bring the glass beneath your faucet.
    • Be sure not to chip or break the glass on the faucet itself.
  4. Turn on the faucet.
    • Pay extra attention as to which knob you are turning. As one is hot, and the other is cold. Turn the cold one. This knob is usually marked with a C or the color blue. If you are red-blue colorblind, ask for assistance from a trusted source (Mom / Dad / Brother / Sister / Spouse / etc.).
  5. As the water line approaches the top of the glass, turn off the faucet completely.
  6. Stir.
  7. If you want this glass of water to be extra special, you can garnish it with an orange slice!
