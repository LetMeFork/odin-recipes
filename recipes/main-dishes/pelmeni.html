Pelmeni

Recipe source(s):

Description

Pelmeni are Russian tiny meat-filled dumplings. Making them by hand with parents is a classic family pastime during the cold and dark winter evenings. In fact, making these tiny meat-filled dumplings is a classic pastime for many Ukrainians. Or, at least, it used to be. These dumplings are a common convenience food. Large batches can be frozen and quickly boiled or fried on demand.
